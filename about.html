Tarraneh Eftekhari

I am a graduate student at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics. My research focuses on transient phenomena, including tidal disruption events and fast radio bursts. In the past, I've worked as part of the Long Wavelength Array collaboration based out of the University of New Mexico.

Though I'm east-coast bound for the time being, I'm a desert dweller at heart, hailing from the enchanted mountains of Northern New Mexico.

You can e-mail me at teftekhari@cfa.harvard.edu.
